How It All Works

Restaurant End

  • Restaurants can sign up for the program.
  • Everyday they must provide meals that include leftover food, simple dishes from leftover ingredients and simple things like rice to make a meal reasonably complete.
  • They can use the money they get for extra ingredients, packaging, fair salary for the staff needed.

User End

  • Users can find their favourite restaurants on the app. Incase they don't, they can talk to the restaurant about signing up for the program.
  • Users donate the amount they want. More research needs to be done to finalize the split of donations between the restaurant, delivery and Dono.
  • There will be rewards (discount coupons to that restaurant) based on donations made to create incentive.

Delivery End

  • We can either create our own delivery network, or partner with a service like Dunzo.
  • We shall partner with NGOs to find places that need food
  • The drivers shall be paid fairly.
  • They shall deliver to places such as orphanages, and individual homeless people on the street.
