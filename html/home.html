Welcome to DONO, a platform where you 'Donate for Rebate'

Our mission is to provide nutritious, quality food to the homeless and while being profitable for both the restaurant and the donors

Our Mission

Reduce Hunger

The Global Hunger Index 2020 ranks India at 101 out of 116 countries in the world. This may not completely solve the hunger crisis, but it's a step in the right direction.

Reduce Food Wastage

Half a pound of food is wasted per meal in restaurants. We cannot do anything about the food left on plates, but the leftovers in the kitchen can be put to good use.

Provide Incentives For Donation

Dono aims to make means to donate towards hunger causes something you use frequently like the discount apps, rather than donating money once a year to charity.
